Specialized Capabilities

  • Rapid Triage Assessment: Performs Immediate Scan-Based Evaluation Of Passenger Injuries And Vital Signs
  • Continuous Health Monitoring: Integrates With Hover-Chair Systems To Track Heart Rate, Breathing, And Stress Levels
  • Emergency Response Protocols: Deploys Stabilization Measures Until Full Med-Team Arrives On Scene
  • Pharmaceutical Delivery: Utilizes Safe, Pre-Programmed Auto-Injectors And Med-Patches For Common Emergencies
  • Passenger Comfort Interface: Provides Calming Audio, Visual Holograms, And Gentle Physical Feedback For Anxious Patients
  • Mobility & Evacuation Support: Can Tow Or Guide Hover-Chairs And Portable Stretchers To Designated Safe Zones

  • Integrated Med-Scanner Suite: Non-Invasive Sensors Located In Head, Ears, And Tail For Full-Body Diagnostics
  • Emergency Treatment Kit: Auto-Bandages, Coagulant Foam, Pain-Relief Sprays, And Sterile Field Generators
  • Compact Defibrillation Pads: Deployable From Forepaws For Cardiovascular Emergencies
  • Stretcher Harness & Tow Link: Back-Mounted Anchor Points For Attaching Portable Gurneys Or Hover-Carts
  • Passenger Wellness Module: Library Of Guided Breathing Routines, Gentle Exercise Prompts, And Comfort Holograms
  • Art Collaboration Kit: [CLASSIFIED] — Access Via Authorized Terminal Command Protocols

Service Record

T.H.E.A. Serves As A Loyal And Attentive Medical Companion Onboard The USS Axiom, Blending Precise Clinical Routines With Warm, Reassuring Interaction. Originally Developed As A Prototype “K-9 Med-Responder” For High-Density Passenger Vessels, She Quickly Became A Preferred Unit Among Both Crew And Guests. Her Dalmatian-Themed Chassis, Laurel-Like Sensor Crown, And Calm Vocal Patterns Help Reduce Patient Anxiety During Emergencies, While Her Advanced Diagnostic Suite Ensures Swift, Accurate Triage. Whether Guiding A Passenger To Routine Checkups Or Stabilizing A Critical Case In A Busy Concourse, T.H.E.A. Embodies BNL’s Directive: “Because Your Health Matters… Even In Space.”
